How MacDu Mango Started

Our Story

MacDu Mango is a food establishment having only a single branch located at 82 Palico Daanan, Bacoor, Cavite. Mr. Julius Dumael the owner who founded MacDu Mango on January 28, 2023, along with his cousin Ms. Melanie Macabante. Inspired by Mangrae, Mr. Dumael pursued creating one for themselves. MacDu is named after the surnames of Mr. Dumael and Ms. Macabante.


The main products of MacDu are fruit shakes, mango overload, milk tea, and halo-halo. We also offer other foods such as pasta, rice dishes, burgers, and Korean dishes. MacDu operates every day from 10:00 am to 9:00 pm. We take orders through walk-in and via Facebook messenger or contact number.

Our Mission

Our mission to achieve a reputation for products of the highest quality and exceptional customer service. We will always strive to give our customers a comprehensive range of quality products and enhance the online shopping experience by improving our platform and providing outstanding customer support.

Our Vision

Our vision is to grow MacDu Mango into the major wholesale supplier of MacDu Mango products in Philippines. Our focus is to become a one stop online shop for retailers where they can purchase bubble tea products through easy seamless online shopping experience.
