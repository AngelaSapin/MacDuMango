Brief Story of MacDu Mango

MacDu Mango is a food establishment having only a single branch located at 82 Palico Daanan, Bacoor, Cavite. Mr. Julius Dumael the owner who founded MacDu Mango on January 28, 2023, along with his cousin Ms. Melanie Macabante. Inspired by Mangrae, a fruit shake business, Mr. Dumael pursued creating one for themselves.
